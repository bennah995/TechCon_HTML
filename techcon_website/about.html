Past Speakers

These are some of the past speakers we have had at previous Techcon events:

Maxwell "Max" Hartman

Bio:

Max is a visionary software architect from NeoCity, known for his early work on decentralized computing platforms. Starting as a self-taught coder, Max rose to fame after launching the open-source project “GridNet,” which revolutionized distributed data storage.

Tech Contribution:

Max pioneered the first scalable peer-to-peer cloud system, enabling data to be shared securely without centralized servers, laying groundwork for modern blockchain tech.

Lena Torres

Bio:

Lena, a robotics engineer from Silicon Harbor, grew up fascinated by human-robot interaction. After completing her PhD in AI, she developed the "EmotiBot" series—robots capable of recognizing and responding to human emotions with unprecedented accuracy.

Contribution:

Her work bridged the gap between AI and empathy, pushing forward the integration of emotional intelligence in everyday robotics, influencing how smart assistants interact with users.

Ezekiel "Zeke" Grant

Bio:

Zeke is a cyber-security guru from the tech underground scene of CyberVille. Known for his relentless hacking skills, he switched sides and became a white-hat hacker after exposing a massive government surveillance program.

Contribution:

Contribution: Zeke invented the "ShadowShield" protocol, a cutting-edge encryption system that became the backbone for secure communications worldwide, protecting millions from data breaches.
